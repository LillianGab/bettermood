Hello, welcome to bettermood.com! This website was created to go along with the visual novel by the name of "Better Mood Club" on renpy, in which we tell the story of a group of teens who navigate their way through their high school by joining a club which supports them through all of their mental health and life challenges. We hope you enjoy our story!
